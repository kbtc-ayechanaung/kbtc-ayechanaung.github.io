THE PHOTOGRAPHER

Thaw Zin Htet, known as Kaung Kaung, is a Burmese documentary and street photographer based in Mae Sot, Thailand. Born in Myanmar, he was forced to flee his homeland due to political upheaval following the 2021 military coup.

Now living as a refugee on the Thai-Myanmar border, Kaung Kaung channels his experiences into powerful visual narratives. His camera becomes his voice — capturing the raw beauty of everyday life, the quiet dignity of displaced communities, and the fierce spirit of resistance.

His work spans street photography, concert documentation, cultural events, and protest movements. Each frame tells a story that newspapers can't — the human moments between the headlines.

"Every moment of truth deserves a witness. I am that witness."
